# How to Make Electronics Assembly Labor

**Top Market Leaders:**
- [Foxconn](/companies/foxconn)
- [Pegatron](/companies/pegatron)
- [Wistron](/companies/wistron)



Skilled workers assemble complex electronics. MacBook assembly: 20-30 stations, 5-8 minutes per unit, requires precision and dexterity.

[Assembly Workers](factory-workers) complete [Training Program](worker-training) (1-2 weeks). Work at [Assembly Line](production-line-equipment) stations. [Quality Control](inspection-staff) inspects. Typical wage: $3-5/hour in China (2025). Benefits, housing provided. Strict quality standards - 99%+ yield required. Repetitive work - ergonomics critical. Workers specialize in specific tasks (cable routing, screw driving, testing).
