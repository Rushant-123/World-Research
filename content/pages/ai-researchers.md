# AI Researchers

## Step 1: Develop interest in AI
Students discover fascination with artificial intelligence through courses, media, or personal projects.

## Step 2: Complete undergraduate education
Pursue bachelor's degree in computer science, mathematics, physics, or related quantitative field.

## Step 3: Study foundational mathematics
Master linear algebra, calculus, probability, statistics, and optimization essential for machine learning.

## Step 4: Learn programming skills
Develop proficiency in Python, C++, and other languages used in AI research and implementation.

## Step 5: Take machine learning courses
Study supervised learning, neural networks, optimization algorithms, and classical ML techniques.

## Step 6: Study deep learning
Learn about convolutional networks, recurrent networks, transformers, and modern architectures.

## Step 7: Complete research projects
Conduct undergraduate research projects applying ML to real problems and developing research skills.

## Step 8: Publish undergraduate work
Present research at undergraduate conferences or workshops to begin building publication record.

## Step 9: Participate in competitions
Compete in Kaggle, NeurIPS competitions, or similar events to develop practical ML skills.

## Step 10: Apply to PhD programs
Submit applications to top computer science PhD programs with strong machine learning groups.
