## Press Frame Construction (Steps 1-25)

1. Design press frame structure using FEA software to calculate stress distribution for 50-1000 ton capacity range

7. Machine reference surfaces on bed plate to achieve flatness within 0.05mm per meter

15. Bore precision holes for hydraulic cylinder mounting with position accuracy +/-0.1mm

18. Machine bed bolster surface flat and parallel to ram within 0.03mm total indicator reading
