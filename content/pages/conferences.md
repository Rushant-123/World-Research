# Technical Conferences and Events Supply Chain

## Conference Research and Selection

1. **Industry Analysis**: Research major technical conferences in target domains (AI/ML, semiconductors, networking, hardware design)
2. **Schedule Compilation**: Create calendar of relevant conferences for upcoming 12-month period
3. **Venue Review**: Evaluate conference locations considering travel logistics and time zones
4. **Reputation Assessment**: Check conference rankings, impact factors, and peer reviews
5. **Past Program Review**: Analyze previous years' programs to assess content quality
6. **Speaker Evaluation**: Review keynote speakers and session leaders from past events
7. **Topic Alignment**: Match conference topics with professional development goals
8. **Format Consideration**: Evaluate in-person vs. hybrid vs. virtual conference formats
9. **Size Assessment**: Consider conference scale (intimate workshops vs. large conventions)
10. **Networking Potential**: Evaluate attendee demographics and networking opportunities

## Budget Planning and Approval

11. **Cost Estimation**: Calculate total conference costs including all expenses
12. **Registration Pricing**: Review early-bird, regular, and late registration fees
13. **Travel Budgeting**: Estimate airfare costs based on departure location and timing
14. **Accommodation Research**: Compare hotel rates near conference venue
15. **Per Diem Calculation**: Determine daily meal and incidental expense allowances
16. **Ground Transportation**: Budget for airport transfers, taxis, and local transit
17. **Additional Fees**: Account for visa fees, travel insurance, and equipment rental
18. **Currency Exchange**: Plan for foreign exchange rates and transaction fees
19. **Budget Justification**: Prepare business case for conference attendance
20. **Approval Process**: Submit conference attendance request to management

## Registration and Payment

21. **Early Bird Timing**: Monitor early registration deadlines for cost savings
22. **Membership Verification**: Check professional society memberships for discounts
23. **Group Rates**: Coordinate with colleagues for team registration discounts
24. **Registration Form**: Complete attendee information and contact details
25. **Dietary Requirements**: Specify meal preferences and restrictions
26. **Accessibility Needs**: Request accommodations for disabilities if needed
27. **Workshop Selection**: Choose pre-conference workshops or tutorials
28. **Tutorial Registration**: Sign up for hands-on training sessions
29. **Payment Processing**: Submit payment via credit card or purchase order
30. **Confirmation Receipt**: Save registration confirmation and receipt

## Travel Arrangements

31. **Flight Booking**: Research and book airline tickets 6-8 weeks in advance
32. **Seat Selection**: Choose preferred seating for optimal comfort
33. **Frequent Flyer**: Apply loyalty program numbers for miles accumulation
34. **Travel Insurance**: Purchase trip cancellation and medical coverage
35. **Visa Application**: Apply for travel visas if attending international conference
36. **Passport Verification**: Ensure passport validity exceeds six months
37. **Hotel Reservation**: Book accommodations at conference hotel or nearby
38. **Room Preferences**: Request quiet room, specific floor, or amenities
39. **Loyalty Programs**: Use hotel rewards programs for points and upgrades
40. **Check-in Dates**: Verify arrival/departure dates match conference schedule

## Pre-Conference Preparation

41. **Agenda Download**: Obtain detailed conference program and schedule
42. **Session Planning**: Mark must-attend presentations and workshops
43. **Speaker Research**: Study backgrounds of key speakers and panelists
44. **Paper Pre-reading**: Download and review accepted papers in advance
45. **Conflict Resolution**: Plan schedule around overlapping sessions of interest
46. **Exhibition Planning**: Review exhibitor list and booth locations
47. **Meeting Scheduling**: Arrange one-on-one meetings with contacts
48. **Networking Goals**: Set specific objectives for connections to make
49. **Elevator Pitch**: Prepare concise introduction of yourself and work
50. **Business Cards**: Order or print 100+ business cards with current information
