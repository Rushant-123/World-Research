# How to Make Tungsten Metal

Tungsten (W): highest melting point of all metals (3422°C). Used in: semiconductors (via plugs), light bulb filaments, tools, armor-piercing projectiles.

## Process:
1. Mine [Tungsten Ore](scheelite-ore) - scheelite (CaWO₄) or wolframite ((Fe,Mn)WO₄)
2. Concentrate by flotation
3. [Chemical Processing](apt-production): convert to ammonium paratungstate (APT) - (NH₄)₁₀H₂(W₂O₇)₆
4. Calcine APT → WO₃ (tungsten trioxide) at 600°C
5. [Hydrogen Reduction](tungsten-reduction): WO₃ + 3H₂ → W + 3H₂O at 800-1000°C
6. Result: tungsten powder
7. [Powder Metallurgy](sintering-process): press powder, sinter at 2000-3000°C
8. Creates solid tungsten billet

**For Semiconductor:** Tungsten deposited by CVD (WF₆ + 3H₂ → W + 6HF). Fills vias connecting metal layers. Resistivity: 5.6 µΩ·cm. Price: $30-50/kg metal, $100-200/kg for sputtering targets.
