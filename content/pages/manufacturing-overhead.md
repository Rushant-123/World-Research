Manufacturing overhead encompasses all indirect costs required to maintain and operate electronics manufacturing facilities. These facilities typically span 500,000 to 2,000,000 square feet and support production volumes of 100,000 to 500,000 units per month. Overhead costs are allocated using direct labor hours, machine hours, or production volume as cost drivers, with typical allocation rates of $85-$150 per direct labor hour.
