2. Open pit or underground mining
3. Oxide ores amenable to heap leaching
4. Sulfide ores require milling and flotation
5. Drilling and blasting
6. Loading and hauling to crusher

13. Centrifugal concentrators
14. Intensive cyanidation of concentrates
15. Flotation for sulfide-hosted gold

21. Oxygen or air sparging

28. Loaded carbon eluted with hot caustic solution
29. Electrowinning from eluate
