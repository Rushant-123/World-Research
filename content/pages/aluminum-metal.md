# How to Make Aluminum Metal

**Top Market Leaders:**
- [Rio Tinto](/companies/rio-tinto) - 18% share
- [Alcoa](/companies/alcoa) - 14% share
- [Norsk Hydro](/companies/norsk-hydro) - 11% share



Aluminum extracted from [Bauxite Ore](bauxite-mining) (Al₂O₃·2H₂O). Two-step process: Bayer Process (bauxite → alumina) + Hall-Héroult Process (alumina → aluminum).

## Bayer Process:
1. Crush bauxite, mix with NaOH at 150-200°C
2. Aluminum oxide dissolves: Al₂O₃ + 2NaOH → 2NaAlO₂ + H₂O
3. Filter out iron oxide, silica (red mud waste)
4. Cool, seed with alumina crystals
5. Al(OH)₃ precipitates
6. Calcine at 1000°C: 2Al(OH)₃ → Al₂O₃ + 3H₂O
7. [Alumina](alumina-production): white powder, 99.5-99.9% Al₂O₃

## Hall-Héroult Process:
1. Dissolve alumina in molten cryolite (Na₃AlF₆) at 960°C in [Electrolysis Cell](hall-heroult-cells)
2. Pass DC current (150,000-300,000 amps!) using [Electricity](electricity)
3. [Carbon Anodes](carbon-anodes) (graphite) oxidize: C + O²⁻ → CO + 2e⁻
4. Cathode (carbon lining): Al³⁺ + 3e⁻ → Al
5. Molten aluminum (melting point 660°C) sinks to bottom
6. Tap every 1-2 days
7. Cast into ingots (25-30 kg) or rolling slabs (10-25 tons)

Energy: 13-15 kWh per kg Al (very energy-intensive). Why aluminum smelters built near hydroelectric dams. Price: $2-3/kg (fluctuates with energy costs).
