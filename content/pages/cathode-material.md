# Lithium-Ion Battery Cathode Material Synthesis

This comprehensive guide covers the industrial-scale synthesis of lithium-ion battery cathode materials including NMC (Nickel Manganese Cobalt), NCA (Nickel Cobalt Aluminum), and LFP (Lithium Iron Phosphate) for high-performance applications such as MacBook batteries.

## Raw Material Preparation and Quality Control

1. Receive nickel sulfate hexahydrate (NiSO4·6H2O) shipment in moisture-controlled warehouse at 20-25°C and <40% relative humidity
2. Verify nickel sulfate purity certificate showing >98.5% purity with <50 ppm iron contamination
3. Conduct incoming quality control testing on nickel sulfate using ICP-OES for metal content verification
4. Store nickel sulfate in sealed polyethylene-lined drums with nitrogen purging to prevent oxidation
5. Receive cobalt sulfate heptahydrate (CoSO4·7H2O) from qualified suppliers with chain-of-custody documentation
6. Test cobalt sulfate for purity (>99.5%) and trace metal contaminants using atomic absorption spectroscopy
7. Verify cobalt sulfate meets automotive-grade specifications with <20 ppm nickel and <10 ppm copper impurities
8. Store cobalt sulfate in dedicated temperature-controlled area at 15-25°C away from reducing agents
9. Receive lithium carbonate (Li2CO3) battery-grade powder with particle size D50 of 8-12 μm
10. Conduct particle size distribution analysis on lithium carbonate using laser diffraction particle analyzer
11. Test lithium carbonate for purity >99.5% and verify sodium content <50 ppm and calcium <20 ppm
12. Store lithium carbonate in moisture-proof containers with desiccant packs in climate-controlled facility
13. Receive manganese sulfate monohydrate (MnSO4·H2O) for NMC synthesis with certified purity >99%
14. Analyze manganese sulfate for heavy metal contamination particularly lead, cadmium, and mercury
15. Verify manganese sulfate particle morphology and dissolution characteristics in deionized water
16. Prepare detailed material inventory system tracking lot numbers, receipt dates, and expiration dates
17. Establish material traceability system linking raw materials to finished cathode batches
18. Set up automated material handling system with barcode scanning for inventory management
19. Install HEPA-filtered clean room environment for powder handling with Class 10,000 cleanliness
20. Calibrate all weighing equipment including precision balances accurate to 0.01g for large batches

29. Weigh cobalt sulfate precisely targeting 1.0 kg per batch for 10% cobalt content in final product

## Quality Control and Certification

The final cathode material must meet stringent specifications:
- Chemical composition: Li1.03Ni0.8Mn0.1Co0.1O2 (±1% tolerance)
- Particle size: D50 = 10-15 μm, D90 < 25 μm
- Tap density: 2.3-2.6 g/cm³
- Specific surface area: 0.3-0.6 m²/g
- Initial discharge capacity: 180-200 mAh/g at C/5 rate (3.0-4.3V)
- First cycle efficiency: >88%
- Capacity retention: >80% after 500 cycles at 1C rate
- Impurities: Na <100 ppm, S <200 ppm, Fe <50 ppm, Ca <50 ppm

This cathode material is suitable for high-performance applications including MacBook batteries, electric vehicles, and energy storage systems.
