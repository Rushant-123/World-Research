22. Use precision applicator to create even TIM coverage pattern

24. Position CPU heatsink over processor aligning mounting holes

26. Tighten heatsink screws in diagonal pattern to specified torque
27. Verify heatsink makes full contact with CPU surface
28. Connect heatsink fan power cable to motherboard CPU_FAN header
